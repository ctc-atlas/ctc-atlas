# CTC-Atlas

**Projects released by the joint laboratory of Raymind Biotechnology Co., Ltd
(睿迈生物) and the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data (深圳大数据研究院 / SRIBD).**

CTC-Atlas builds single-cell foundation models and data infrastructure for
liquid-biopsy oncology — turning circulating tumor cells (CTCs) and other
single-cell readouts into clinically actionable insight.

---

## Who we are

This GitHub account hosts projects released by **CTC-Atlas**, a joint lab
co-founded by **Raymind Biotechnology Co., Ltd (Raymind)** and the **Shenzhen
Research Institute of Big Data (SRIBD)**. It combines Raymind's wet-lab and
clinical strengths in circulating tumor cells with SRIBD's expertise in
large-scale data and AI foundation models.

## What we are building

- **Data** — curated private and public single-cell transcriptomic datasets
  spanning CTCs, white blood cells, and tumor references.
- **Models** — pretrained and fine-tuned single-cell foundation models
  (e.g. *ModulaFM*) for cell annotation, tumor-cell identification,
  perturbation and drug-response prediction.
- **Translation** — pipelines that bring these capabilities from raw
  sequencing data to clinical decision support.

## Our vision

We are exploring a **data-sharing framework for multi-center hospitals and
research institutions**, working toward a unified, secure, and efficient
multi-party collaboration platform.

Through open collaboration, we aim to cultivate high-impact application
scenarios — **cancer screening, precision oncology, and drug discovery** —
and to build an integrated life-health ecosystem that unites **data
resources, model capabilities, and clinical translation**.

Our goal is to contribute to the high-quality growth of the life-health
industry in Shenzhen, the Greater Bay Area, and China as a whole.
